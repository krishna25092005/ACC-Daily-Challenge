Sample Input

Input: arr = [-1, 0, 1, 2, -1, -4]
Output: [[-1, 1]]
Explanation: arr[0] + arr[2] = (-1)+ 1 = 0.
arr[2] + arr[4] = 1 + (-1) = 0.
The distinct triplets are [-1,1].
